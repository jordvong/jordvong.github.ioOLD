Fishbowl is an open-mic event held every week at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth. Students, faculty and community members come and hear/tell true stories based on a theme. The topic changes every week, bringing a brand new energy to each meeting. We are an all-inclusive, respecting and caring organization that sees the potential in forming relationships and skills through stories.
Interested in attending a Fishbowl meeting and hearing true stories or even telling your own? Check out our Facebook page!
